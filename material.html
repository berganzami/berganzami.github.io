Some useful (online) bibliographic material

In this section a list of on-line reviews and basic references on the topics I have mainly studied is presented. Also my CiteULike account may be useful.

  1. Statmec

    2. General stuff: Il Trattatello di probabilit√†, the Gallavotti's review on equilibrium statmec. Check also the book on statmec by Sethna, the introduction to statmech in Velasco's notes. See also Monasson's lectures. Kinetics of phase transitions by Klein et al. First-order phase transitions review by Binder. Casalbuoni's notes on field theory and critical phenomena. Large deviations in statmec by Touchette.

    Disorder: The spin-glass and supercooled liquids reviews by Cavagna. See also the review "Theories for the glass transition" by Schilling.

    Graphs: Newman's review on complex graphs. Albert-Barabasi review.

    Interdisciplinar: Peliti's review on statmec of Darwinian evolution, and the review by Castellano et. al. on statmec of social dynamics.

  2. Quantum

    3. General On superconductivity and superfluidity, c.f.: FM Marchetti's notes, Eschrig's notes on superconductivity. Path integrals with some examples by MacKenzie. The Volovik book. Legget review on 3He. An introduction to quantum field theory: Srednicki book. See also Tong's lectures, Kaspers site, and Cappelli's notes. Monasson's course notes.

    CFT: Ginsparg's Les Houches review, Cardy's 2008 Les Houches lectures, and Senechal review on bosonization. Luttinger Liquid notes by Giamarchi.

    Entanglement: Amico et. al. review on entanglement in many-body, and Eisert et. al. review on the area law. Cardy & Calabrese first paper on entanglement in CFT (2004). Holzhey et. al. 1994 paper. Preskill's notes on quantum information.

    Integrability: Andrei's lessons, Baxter's book, and Franchini's notes.

    Numerical methods: El Escorial book (with a review on dmrg), Javi Laguna's thesis, Lauchli talks on numerical methods (useful for references), tensor product states by Cirac and Verstraete. And Nishino's site.
